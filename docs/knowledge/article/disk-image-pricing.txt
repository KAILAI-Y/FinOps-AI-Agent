# Disk and image pricing lifecycle notes

Source: https://cloud.google.com/compute/disks-image-pricing

Disk pricing basics

Each VM has at least one disk attached to it. Each disk incurs a cost.

All disk-related charges are prorated by seconds.

Provisioned disk space includes all used and unused space.

If you provision a disk, you are billed for the entire provisioned disk space until you relinquish it.

Persistent disk lifecycle implication

Disk pricing is separate from VM instance pricing.

A terminated VM can therefore still leave behind cost if its persistent disks or snapshots still exist.

Operational interpretation

When reviewing a terminated VM for lingering cost, check whether persistent disks, boot disks, or snapshots still exist and are still provisioned, because those resources continue to be billed until they are deleted.
